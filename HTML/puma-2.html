Puma Inhale Color Shifting

The Inhale Color Shifting revives a bold turn-of-the-millennium silhouette with futuristic flair, merging retro energy with modern innovation. Designed for impact, it features a sculpted EVA midsole with cushioning tuned for everyday comfort and street-ready stability. The outsole uses a high-abrasion rubber compound for lasting grip and traction across urban terrain. Up top, the Inhale stands out with its dynamic wave-like overlays and a color-shifting upper that changes with the light—giving the shoe a distinct look from every angle. Breathable mesh underlays ensure ventilation, while the reflective accents add function and flair. This eye-catching revival redefines Y2K performance aesthetics with a fearless, fashion-forward twist.
