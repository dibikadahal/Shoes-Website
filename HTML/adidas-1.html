Adidas Predator Megaride

Slip into a slice of soccer history. The Predator Megaride shoes pay tribute to the iconic Predator designs of the 2000s. An anodized synthetic leather upper with mesh underlays provides breathability and comfort, while molded inserts add texture. Megaride cushioning puts a spring in your step for all-day comfort. Fold over the tongue and lace up — the external heel counter locks in your foot for a secure fit. Echoing the cleats once worn by legends, these lifestyle shoes let you make a statement wherever the day takes you.
