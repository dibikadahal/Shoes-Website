Asics JAPAN PRO

The ASICS Japan Pro, a low-top sneaker with a vintage court-inspired design. It features a soft tan suede upper that gives it a warm, earthy look. The ASICS stripes are done in a contrasting black leather, adding a bold touch to the side panels. The inner lining is a deep green, offering a subtle pop of color. The shoe is finished with a classic gum rubber sole, which not only enhances grip but also complements the overall retro style. With its mix of suede texture and classic color contrasts, the Japan Pro blends casual comfort with timeless streetwear appeal.
